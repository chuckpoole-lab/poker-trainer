Notes

Option A (Current): Cool charcoal grays with purple accent cards. Functional but feels like a dark-mode developer tool. No warmth.

Option B (Poker Lounge): The background shifts to a dark forest green that echoes the poker felt from your table screens. Gold highlights for streaks and achievements. Feels like you're sitting at a table in a well-lit card room.

Option C (Whiskey & Felt): Rich brown tones like leather and wood paneling, with emerald green pops. The warmest option — feels like a classic card room with dark wood furniture. Pairs naturally with the brown rail on your poker table.

Option D (Card Room Gold): Dark navy base with gold as the primary accent. More premium/luxury feel. Less "cozy" than B or C, but feels high-stakes and aspirational.

Also note: Options B, C, and D consolidate Home and Play into a single "Play" nav item, freeing up a slot. This matches what users expect — one tap to get to the action.
