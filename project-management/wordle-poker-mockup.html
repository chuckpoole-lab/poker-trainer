Key Design Decisions

Light, warm background (#faf8f5) instead of dark charcoal — feels welcoming, not intimidating. The warmth comes from the cream undertone, not darkness.

Felt green (#4a7c59) is the primary accent — connects every screen to the poker table. Used for CTAs, active states, and the daily banner.

5-tab nav instead of 8 — Home, Play, Train, Progress, More. Every tab has a distinct job. No duplicates. "Train" consolidates Learn + Assess + Drills into one focused launcher.

Home is a real landing page — shows your streak, promotes the daily challenge, offers quick actions ("What do you want to work on?"), and shows what's new. It answers the question "why should I open this today?"

Train is the serious player's hub — no tabs to click through. Pick your focus, see the time commitment, get right to work. "Fix My Leaks" uses your assessment data to recommend what to drill.

Feedback is 4 questions max — first time? fun? coming back? suggestions. That's it. Takes 30 seconds. Much more likely to get responses.
